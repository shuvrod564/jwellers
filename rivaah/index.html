What Makes a Telugu Bride Shine?

Nichayathartham

Nichayathartham is a time for family and friends to come together and celebrate the couple's upcoming union. With delicate jewellery and a more subtle color palette, a more understated yet elegant and graceful is precisely what this occasion calls for.

Nalangu Ceremony

This exquisite Nalangu trousseau features the haaram, vanki, bangles, and jhumki, with intricate gold work and beautiful pink and green stones. The perfect finishing touches include a classic papidi billa and breathtaking neckwear with the Laxmi motif.

Muhurtham Ceremony

The Muhurtham ceremony is a pivotal event in any Telugu bride's life. Every detail of her attire, from the intricate embroidery on her stunning saree to the delicate craftsmanship of her jewellery, is meticulously chosen to ensure she looks and feels her best on this milestone of an occasion.

Reception

A grand reception also calls for dazzling attire and stunning jewellery sets. The bride's ensemble typically features intricate designs, luxurious fabrics, and precious gemstones. The jewellery, including bangles, necklaces, and earrings, is often tastefully crafted with intricate goldwork, diamonds, emeralds, and other precious stones. The overall effect is a Telugu bridal look of grandeur, elegance, and timeless beauty.
